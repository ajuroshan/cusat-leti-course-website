About The Program

Master's Degree Program "New generation of electronic component base"

A cutting-edge elective program tailored for students passionate about electronic component design and semiconductors. This program offers multi-department collaboration, welcoming students from various engineering and science disciplines to promote interdisciplinary learning. Additionally, students have the opportunity to study abroad, spending one year at St. Petersburg Electrotechnical University 'LETI' to gain global exposure and enhance their academic experience.

Syllabus:

Semester 1
  • Computer Technology and Simulation in Electronics (3 credits - online)
  • Microprocessor Techniques (4 credits)
  • Problems of Modern Electronics (4 credits)
  • Elective: Russian/Foreign Language (2 credits)
  • Basics of Scientific Research (2 credits)
  • Elective: X-ray structural methods / Laser Physics (3 credits)
Semester 3
  • Russian/Foreign Language (2 credits)
  • Integral and Fibre Optics (4 credits)
  • Semiconductor Heterostructure Technology (3 credits)
  • Computer Simulation and Design of Microwave/Optical Devices (4 credits)
  • Elective: Laser Fundamentals / Topological IC Design (4 credits)
  • Elective: CAD Systems / Photonics Materials (4 credits)
Semester 2
  • Micro- and Nanotechnology Processes (4 credits)
  • Russian/Foreign Language (2 credits)
  • Basic Technologies for IC Manufacturing (3 credits)
  • Semiconductor Heterostructure Technology (3 credits)
  • Elective: Digital IC Design / Electronics Diagnostics (4 credits)
  • Economic Elective: R&D Commercialisation / Foreign Economic Activity (2 credits)
Semester 4
  • Practice and Internships at Partner Facilities
  • Master Thesis Preparation and Defense

Focus of Interest:

  • Design and creation of modern electronic component base elements.
  • Basic technological routes and parameters of microelectronic production processes.
  • Basics of material science of semiconductors and heterostructures.
  • Types of defects in microelectronic product production.
  • Basic technological, control-measuring, and auxiliary equipment for microelectronic production.
  • Design of devices, appliances, and systems of electronic technics based on given requirements.
  • Characteristics of modern CAD systems for microelectronics.
  • Methods for solving problems in technological and circuit design of electronic components.

Saint Petersburg Electrotechnical University "LETI"

About LETI

Saint Petersburg Electrotechnical University "LETI" is one of the world's largest education centers specializing in radio engineering, electrical engineering, electronics and computer science. University graduates get Bachelor's and Master's degree or specialist qualification. Today the University trains around 9000 students. Over 200 professors and PhDs work in the University. ETU "LETI" provides education in 19 scientific fields for Bachelors and 53 educational programs for Masters.

Historical Achievements

Saint Petersburg Electrotechnical University is a participant of the Priority 2030 program and ranks 5th in graduate salaries among St. Petersburg technical universities. It is recognized in the Three Missions of the University Russia (2023) and Forbes Russia (2023) rankings. The university’s programs are acclaimed as "The best educational programs of innovative Russia" and it holds notable positions in the RUR Governance and Management ranking (2023) and RAEX ranking for information technology. It also excels in the AC Expert subject rating for artificial intelligence.
